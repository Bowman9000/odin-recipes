Hamburger

Description

Making a delicious hamburger at home is easier than you might think. Don't let the layers of flavors scare you off — give this homemade hamburger recipe a try today!

Ingredients

  • Buns: 4 hamburger buns
  • Cheese: 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • Condiments: Ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise
  • Lettuce: 1 pound ground beef (80% lean)
  • Meat: ½ pound ground pork, ½ pound lean ground beef
  • Onion: 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • Pickles: 8 pickle slices
  • Salt and pepper: To taste
  • Tomato: 1 large tomato, sliced

How to cook

  1. Prepare the patties: In a bowl, season the ground beef with salt and pepper. Divide the meat into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties.
  2. Cook the patties: Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat. Cook the patties for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired doneness. Add a slice of cheddar cheese on top of each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  3. Toast the buns: While the patties are cooking, lightly toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  4. Assemble the hamburgers: Spread your preferred condiments on the bottom half of each bun. Place a lettuce leaf on top, followed by the cooked patty with melted cheese. Add slices of tomato, onion, and pickles. Top with the other half of the bun.
  5. Serve and enjoy: Serve the hamburgers immediately with your favorite sides.
