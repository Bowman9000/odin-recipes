Sushi

Description

Making sushi at home is easier than you might think. Don't let the rolling process scare you off — give this homemade sushi recipe a try today!

Ingredients

  • Rice: 2 cups sushi rice
  • Rice vinegar: 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • Sugar: 2 tablespoons sugar
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon salt
  • Nori: 5 sheets of nori (seaweed)
  • Fish: 1/2 pound sushi-grade salmon or tuna, thinly sliced
  • Vegetables: 1 cucumber, julienned; 1 avocado, sliced
  • Condiments: Soy sauce, wasabi, pickled ginger

How to cook

  1. Prepare the sushi rice: Rinse the sushi rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Cook the rice according to the package instructions. Once cooked, transfer the rice to a large bowl. In a small bowl, mix the rice vinegar, sugar, and salt until dissolved. Pour the mixture over the rice and gently fold to combine. Let the rice cool to room temperature.
  2. Prepare the fillings: While the rice is cooling, prepare your fillings. Slice the fish into thin strips, and julienne the cucumber and avocado.
  3. Assemble the sushi: Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat, shiny side down. Wet your hands to prevent sticking, then spread a thin layer of sushi rice over the nori, leaving a 1-inch border at the top. Arrange the fish and vegetables in a line across the center of the rice.
  4. Roll the sushi: Using the bamboo mat, carefully roll the sushi away from you, pressing gently to shape the roll. Seal the edge with a little water. Repeat with the remaining ingredients.
  5. Slice and serve: Using a sharp knife, slice the sushi roll into bite-sized pieces. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
